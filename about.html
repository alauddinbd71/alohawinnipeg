Our Story

Built on Honesty. Proven by Results.

Aloha Plumbing & Drain Services was built on a simple belief: Winnipeg homeowners deserve honest plumbing advice — not just expensive solutions that benefit the contractor.

Too many customers had been told they needed to dig up their yard, replace their entire sewer, or pay for services they didn't need. Julian and the Aloha team set out to change that by investing in the best technology and the highest level of pipe relining certification available anywhere in Canada.

Today, Aloha is the only Double Master Certified pipe reliner in the country. That means when we look at your sewer line, we see options no other company can offer. Our CCTV camera system lets you see exactly what's happening inside your pipes — and our flat-rate pricing means you always know what you'll pay before we start.

Whether it's a blocked kitchen drain or a collapsed main sewer line, Aloha shows up, figures it out, and fixes it right. We've traveled 50+ km outside Winnipeg for jobs other companies wouldn't touch — because that's who we are.

🏆 CANADA'S ONLY DOUBLE MASTER CERTIFIED PIPE RELINER

This elite certification is the highest level of training available in CIPP (Cured-in-Place Pipe) trenchless relining. No other company in Canada holds this certification — which means no other company can offer the same level of quality, expertise, and confidence in the result.
