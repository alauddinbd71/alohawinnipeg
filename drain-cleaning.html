Winnipeg's Root Problem

Winnipeg's older neighborhoods — River Heights, St. Vital, Transcona, and others — are filled with mature trees whose roots seek out your sewer pipes. Root intrusion is the #1 cause of recurring drain blockages in the city.

Most plumbers will snake through the roots temporarily. Aloha uses industrial-grade root cutting and camera verification to ensure the job is done completely — and we'll tell you honestly if the roots will return and what your long-term options are.

How Do I Know If I Have a Root Problem?

  • Slow drains that keep coming back even after snaking
  • Gurgling sounds from toilet or floor drains
  • Multiple fixtures backing up at the same time
  • Sewage smell in your basement
  • Your home is 30+ years old with mature trees nearby
