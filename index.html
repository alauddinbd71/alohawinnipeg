Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

Do you charge extra for emergency calls?
We believe in transparent, flat-rate pricing. Our pricing is listed on our website and what we quote is what you pay — no surprise emergency surcharges or hidden fees.
What is pipe relining and will you dig up my yard?
CIPP pipe relining is a no-dig solution that creates a brand-new pipe inside your existing one. We insert a liner through small access points — no excavation, no torn-up lawn, no mess. The result lasts 50+ years.
How quickly can you come out?
Many customers report we arrive within 1–2 hours for emergency situations. Same-day service is our standard. We also offer scheduled appointments for non-emergency work the next day.
What is a free camera inspection?
We insert a CCTV camera into your sewer or drain line so you can watch live footage of what's happening inside your pipes. It's completely free and helps us give you an honest assessment before recommending any work.
Another company said I need to dig up my sewer — is that true?
Not always. Many customers come to us after getting expensive excavation quotes and we're able to reline or repair the pipe without any digging. We've saved customers $2,000–$13,000 over competitor quotes. Call us for a second opinion.
Do you serve areas outside Winnipeg?
Yes — we are a licensed sewer and water contractor in Winnipeg, Selkirk, and Brandon. We've also traveled 50+ km outside the city for jobs that other companies couldn't solve. Call us and we'll confirm your area.
What makes Aloha different from other plumbers?
We're Canada's only Double Master Certified pipe reliner. We use live camera inspection on every job. We're known for solving problems others couldn't fix, saving customers thousands, and charging exactly what we quote.
Can I get a sewer inspection when buying a home?
Absolutely — this is one of our most popular services for homebuyers. We've done free sewer scopes on the day of possession to give buyers peace of mind before they sign. Ask about our pre-purchase inspection service.
