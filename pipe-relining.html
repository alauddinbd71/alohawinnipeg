The Process

How CIPP Pipe Relining Works — Step by Step

1

Camera Inspection

We insert our CCTV camera to assess the pipe condition. You watch live footage and we explain exactly what's happening and whether relining is the right solution.

2

Pipe Cleaning & Preparation

We thoroughly clean and prepare the existing pipe — removing roots, debris, and scale — to ensure perfect adhesion for the liner.

3

Liner Insertion

A flexible liner saturated with our special epoxy resin is carefully inserted into the damaged pipe through small existing access points — no excavation needed.

4

Curing (Hardening)

The liner is inflated and cured using hot water or UV light, hardening into a seamless, jointless pipe inside your existing one.

5

Final Camera Verification

We perform a final camera inspection so you can see your brand-new pipe lining with your own eyes — before we leave. You get proof on USB.
